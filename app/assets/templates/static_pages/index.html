WHO ARE WE

Hong Kong Student Association (HKSA) at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor is founded in 1990 by a group of Hong Kong students who wants to promote HK culture and enhance bonding among HK students. With over 250 members, HKSA strives to organize a wide variety of events and functions for students to participate, regardless of members and non-members.
