WHO ARE WE

Hong Kong Student Association (HKSA) at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor is founded in 1990 by a group of Hong Kong students who want to promote HK culture and enhance bonding among HK students. With over 250 members, HKSA strives to organize a wide variety of events and functions for students to participate, regardless of members and non-members.

WHAT WE DO

Promote HK Culture

We expose people to the interesting cultures of Hong Kong, be it its food, its musics, or its movies

Social Event

We host social event - let people mingle and build lasting friendships

Career Networking

Join our career workshops to network with alumni and build professional network
